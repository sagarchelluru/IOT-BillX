Telemetry Flow

Smart meters capture voltage, current, frequency, and cumulative energy values every 5 seconds. Payloads are signed with embedded TPM keys before they leave the premise, ensuring authenticity.

  • MQTT over TLS 1.3 with device mutual auth
  • Edge inference for anomaly detection
  • WASM validator filters bad packets

Blockchain Anchoring

Aggregated readings are bundled into Merkle proofs and submitted to the Ethereum smart contract. Each settlement event stores usage, tariff hash, and signature references immutably.
