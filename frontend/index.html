System Overview

IoT meters stream signed telemetry packets (voltage, current, kWh) to edge gateways. Gateways validate signatures, aggregate usage, and anchor results to the Ethereum blockchain through our billing smart contract. Each settlement event emits immutable usage receipts that consumers, utilities, and auditors can verify instantly.

  • Cryptographic proofs of consumption
  • Programmable tariffs and incentives
  • Fraud-resistant and transparent billing
